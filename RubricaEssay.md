```markdown
# Essay Rubric

**Total: 5 Points Possible**

---

## 1. Introduction  
**Maximum score:** 0.7 pts  
**Rating Scale:** 5

| Level | Descriptor | Score |
|---|---|---|
| 4 - Excellent | Engaging hook; concise, relevant background; thesis explicitly names two distinct arguments; smooth, purposeful transition to Body 1; clear, coherent flow. | 0.7 pts |
| 3 - Good | Solid hook; background mostly relevant; thesis states two arguments, minor vagueness; transition present; generally coherent. | 0.56 pts |
| 2 - Satisfactory | Basic hook or background, somewhat generic; thesis present but one argument vague/implicit; transition abrupt; coherence uneven. | 0.42 pts |
| 1 - Needs Improvement | Weak/irrelevant hook/background; thesis unclear or lists only one argument; missing/weak transition; flow choppy. | 0.28 pts |
| 0 - Poor | No clear hook/background; no workable thesis; no transition; disorganized. | 0.14 pts |

---

## 2. Body 1  
**Maximum score:** 1 pt  
**Rating Scale:** 5

| Level | Descriptor | Score |
|---|---|---|
| 4 - Excellent | Focused topic sentence aligns to Argument #1; specific, credible evidence; warrant clearly explains how evidence supports the claim; strong concluding sentence; paragraph is cohesive. | 1 pt |
| 3 - Good | Clear topic sentence; relevant evidence, slightly general; warrant present but could be deeper; concluding sentence present; mostly coherent. | 0.8 pts |
| 2 - Satisfactory | Topic sentence somewhat broad; evidence present but thin/partly relevant; warrant incomplete or assumed; basic wrap-up; coherence uneven. | 0.6 pts |
| 1 - Needs Improvement | Vague topic sentence; weak/irrelevant evidence; warrant missing/unclear; no real conclusion; poor organization. | 0.4 pts |
| 0 - Poor | No topic sentence; no usable evidence; no warrant; no conclusion; incoherent. | 0.2 pts |

---

## 3. Body 2  
**Maximum score:** 1 pt  
**Rating Scale:** 5

| Level | Descriptor | Score |
|---|---|---|
| 4 - Excellent | Focused topic sentence aligns to Argument #2; specific, credible evidence; warrant clearly explains how evidence supports the claim; strong concluding sentence; paragraph is cohesive. | 1 pt |
| 3 - Good | Clear topic sentence; relevant evidence, slightly general; warrant present but could be deeper; concluding sentence present; mostly coherent. | 0.8 pts |
| 2 - Satisfactory | Topic sentence somewhat broad; evidence present but thin/partly relevant; warrant incomplete or assumed; basic wrap-up; coherence uneven. | 0.6 pts |
| 1 - Needs Improvement | Vague topic sentence; weak/irrelevant evidence; warrant missing/unclear; no real conclusion; poor organization. | 0.4 pts |
| 0 - Poor | No topic sentence; no usable evidence; no warrant; no conclusion; incoherent. | 0.2 pts |

---

## 4. Counter-argument  
**Maximum score:** 1 pt  
**Rating Scale:** 5

| Level | Descriptor | Score |
|---|---|---|
| 4 - Excellent | Fairly presents a real counter-argument, offers a clear, logical rebuttal supported by specific evidence; warrant explains why rebuttal outweighs the counter-point; strong concluding sentence; highly coherent. | 1 pt |
| 3 - Good | Real counter-argument; rebuttal reasonable with relevant evidence; warrant present but could be deeper; concluding sentence present; coherent overall. | 0.8 pts |
| 2 - Satisfactory | Counter-argument simplified or straw-manned; rebuttal general with limited evidence; warrant thin; basic wrap-up; uneven coherence. | 0.6 pts |
| 1 - Needs Improvement | Counter-argument unclear/irrelevant; rebuttal weak or mostly opinion; little/no evidence; warrant missing; poor organization. | 0.4 pts |
| 0 - Poor | No meaningful counter-argument or rebuttal; no evidence; incoherent. | 0.2 pts |

---

## 5. Conclusion  
**Maximum score:** 0.5 pts  
**Rating Scale:** 5

| Level | Descriptor | Score |
|---|---|---|
| 4 - Excellent | Restates thesis freshly, two arguments still clear; concisely synthesizes key points, and delivers a purposeful call to action or thoughtful final reflection; strong sense of closure; coherent. | 0.5 pts |
| 3 - Good | Restates thesis with minor repetition; summarizes main points; call to action/reflection present but mild; coherent close. | 0.4 pts |
| 2 - Satisfactory | Basic restatement, repetitive; partial summary; brief/generic closing move; coherence adequate. | 0.3 pts |
| 1 - Needs Improvement | Vague or missing restatement; weak summary; closing idea unclear; disorganized. | 0.2 pts |
| 0 - Poor | No real conclusion; introduces new arguments; incoherent. | 0.1 pts |

---

## 6. Grammar & Mechanics  
**Maximum score:** 0.8 pts  
**Rating Scale:** 5

| Level | Descriptor | Score |
|---|---|---|
| 4 - Excellent | Polished prose: varied, appropriate vocabulary; precise grammar and punctuation; 0–2 minor errors that do not affect clarity or tone. | 0.8 pts |
| 3 - Good | Generally correct; minor, infrequent errors, 3–5, with little impact on clarity; vocabulary appropriate. | 0.64 pts |
| 2 - Satisfactory | Noticeable errors, 6–8, that sometimes distract; vocabulary occasionally repetitive or slightly off-register; overall understandable. | 0.48 pts |
| 1 - Needs Improvement | Frequent errors, 9–12, that disrupt clarity/flow; limited or unsuitable vocabulary. | 0.32 pts |
| 0 - Poor | Pervasive errors, 13+; meaning often unclear; severely impedes readability. | 0.16 pts |
```
